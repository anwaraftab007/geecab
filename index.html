Products

Our Products

Wires and Cables

We manufacture high-quality wires and cables from pure copper for 100% conductivity. Our range includes FR/FRLS cables, CCTV cables, CAT-5 cables, and PVC submersible cables for both residential and industrial use.

PVC Electrical Pipes and Casing

We manufacture high-quality PVC conduit pipes in sizes from 20mm to 63mm, ideal for wiring and fittings. Our pipes are corrosion-resistant, fire-resistant, and easy to cut, ensuring long-lasting and flexible performance.

Extension cords

We manufacture durable extension cords with 1 three-pin socket and 3 two-pin sockets, rated for 5AMP, 250V, and 5 meters in length. Our cords are lightweight, strong, and designed for frequent use, ensuring safety and reliability.

MCB Destribution Box

We manufacture durable MCB distribution boxes with copper current-carrying parts for improved conductivity. Coated with powder coating for long-lasting service, they come in various sizes and are widely used across India.

PVC Tapes

We manufacture durable PVC tapes with excellent insulation properties, protecting against high voltage and water. Available in various colors and lengths, they are widely used across India.

Electric Meters

We manufacture a variety of electric meters, including Single and Three Phase Smart and Static Energy Meters, designed for accuracy and durability. Available across India through our extensive network.

Gang Boxes

We manufacture Nano Boxes (Gang Boxes) in sizes from 1 Way to 8 Way, offering durability and compatibility with modern switches and sockets. Available in white, they provide long-lasting performance and are widely distributed across India.
