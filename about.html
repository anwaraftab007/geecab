We Build Effective Electric Circulation in your house.

Ganayak Industries has been a leading electrical appliance manufacturer in India for over 20 years. We started our operations in 2001, with wires and cables as its primary product. Since then, we have widened our product range to PVC (Conduit) Pipes, MCB Box, Extension cords, PVC Tapes, Electric Meters, Nano Box, and more.

We are an ISO 9001: 2015 & ISO 14001: 2015 certified company offering products as per different Indian & world standards. GeeCab Ltd. was first set up in Bada Bazaar, Calcutta, majorly trading in the Eastern and North-Eastern states. Since then, we have developed a PAN India presence. We also deal internationally in Bhutan.

We provide a wide range of cabling solutions to almost all sectors of the economy including Power, Oil & Gas, petrochemical, steel, Industrial & Housing sectors. Our vast wire and cables catalog includes Copper and PVC Wires, Single core and Multicore Industrial Cables, Submersible Cable, Telephone Cables, etc.

Our manufacturing plants are well equipped with all modern machinery to manufacture Quality Products. We are proud of our phenomenal distribution channel.

Our Mission

Our mission is to expand and continually improve the quality practices and employ an experienced workforce to manufacture electrical appliances of GeeCab Ltd., both in the domestic and international markets, that result in customer satisfaction as well as employee satisfaction. Our goal is to provide the highest standards and services through skilled personnel and management.

Our Vision

Our vision is driven by the goal of carving Ganayak Industries as the largest provider of electrical appliances inclusive of high-skill technical expertise.
